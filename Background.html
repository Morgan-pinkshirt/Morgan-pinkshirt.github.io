May 1915 - Italy enters WW1 on the side of France and Britain1.
Italy does not get the territories promised by the Treaty of Versailles, so tensions increase2.
Protests and revolts are frequent in Italy due to the large number of casualties suffered during the war and to a feeling of betrayal because the promised territories by the Secret Treaty of London of 1915 were not gained3.
The coalition governments are weak, they “do little except repress the strike movements by force”4.
